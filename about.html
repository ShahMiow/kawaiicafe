Welcome To The Kawaii Café

The Kawaii Café is situated in Tallaght, very close to the Technological University of Dublin (Tallaght Campus). Our goal is to make student's life easier by providing a wonderful place to relax and enjoy a masterfully crafted beverage or cake.

The Kawaii Café started as a passion project of a few friends. During college, we spend a lot of our free time in Café places, but we found that none of them were what we were looking for. We started a discussion about what the ideal Café looks like, and so, Kawaii Café was born.


We wanted to created a place which looks very neat, while also being the perfect cozy place to relax after a long day of College. While this was important for us, we also wanted to make sure that the products we sell are of a very high quality.

As we put more and more ideas together, Kawaii Café turned from a passion project to an actual business idea. You are currently on the website we made for the Café, and I hope that you will find it useful.
